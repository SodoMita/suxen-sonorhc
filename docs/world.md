# Chrono Nexus: World Documentation

This document explains the setting, physical rules, environments, and the core structural layers of the **Chrono Nexus**.

---

## 🌀 Physical Rules & Dimensions

### 1. The Core Resonance
The center of the Nexus is powered by a massive rotating crystal core. The core shapes surrounding space based on the emotional and physical stability of those inside it.

### 2. Dimensional Instability & Costume Fluctuation
The rifts generate high localized electromagnetic currents. These currents disrupt physical and holographic layers, causing character outfits (hologram stabilizers, healing silks, and loose robes) to shift, cling, or partially phase when characters step into high-tension fields or close-range contact.

### 3. The Bonding Loop
The gravity of the Nexus naturally pulls compatible souls together. As trust and affinity increase, the dimensional fields around the characters stabilize, calming the environmental rifts while simultaneously unlocking closer, more intense physical perspectives and soft, flushed facial portraits.
